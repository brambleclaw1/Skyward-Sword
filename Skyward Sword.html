Skyward Sword

All about Skyward Sword

Skyward Sword originally came out on the Wii in 2011.
It could only be played with the Wii Remote Plus, a special
type of remote for the motion controls. It was ported to
Nintendo Switch in 2021, using only the normal Joy Cons.
